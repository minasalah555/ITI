About this Course

The demand for highly skilled leaders is growing in today’s fast-evolving business landscape. Executives need a holistic skill set to advance their careers and build resilient, high-performing organisations. The Oxford Senior Leadership Executive Programme combines content from three leading online programmes from Saïd Business School, University of Oxford, to provide you with leadership, strategy, and resilience skills to ensure your organisation thrives. If you want to build resilient, high-performing organisations, this programme is for you. Over 15 weeks, you’ll learn how to align purpose with strategy, train teams to anticipate and respond to uncertainty, and drive long-term business success.

The Oxford Senior Leadership Executive Programme is certified by the United Kingdom CPD Certification Service and may be applicable to individuals who are members of, or are associated with, UK-based professional bodies. The programme has an estimated 200 hours of learning. Note: should you wish to claim CPD activity, the onus is on you. About the certificate

Learn about strategic leadership and earn an executive certificate from Saïd Business School, University of Oxford. Assessment is continuous and based on a series of practical assignments completed online. In order to be issued with your digital certificate, you’ll need to meet the requirements outlined in the programme handbook. The handbook will be made available to you as soon as you begin the programme.

Your certificate will be issued in your legal name and sent to you digitally upon successful completion of the program, as per the stipulated requirements.
